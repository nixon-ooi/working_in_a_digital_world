1.0 Project Workflow & Requirements

I would need the following resources complete the project:

  • Primary Research on colonial effects on: Phillipines, Brazil & India
  • Visual Assets: Archival imagery of colonial artifacts and historical documents
  • Software: Adobe Illustrator/Figma

2.0 Project Schedule: 3-Week Timeline

This timeline reflects a "Research-through-Design" approach (Stappers and Giaccardi, 2017), where the final poster acts as the culmination of my literary and archival synthesis.

Phase Task Outcome
Week 1: Synthesis Research and select 8 specific artifacts for each clock face. Draft Annotated Bibliography
Week 2: Layout Construct the "Layered Clock" central graphic. Integrate the 12, 3, 6, 9 digital anchors with the hand-drawn colonial artifacts. Initial Visual Prototype
Week 3: Refinement Refine the "Legend" and "Legend entries." Ensure the critique of "Temporal Colonialism" is clear through the visual hierarchy. Peer Review & Legibility Test
